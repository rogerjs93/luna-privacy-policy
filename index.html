🔒 Your Data, Your Control

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Luna ("the App") is developed as a privacy-first menstrual cycle tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Luna stores all personal data locally on your device by default. This includes:

  • Cycle logs (period dates, flow intensity)
  • Mood and symptom entries
  • Personal notes
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Optional Google Drive Sync

Luna offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. If you choose to enable it:

  • You sign in with your own Google account.
  • Your cycle data is encrypted and stored as a file in your personal Google Drive (in the app-specific folder).
  • Luna only accesses its own app folder — it cannot read any other files in your Drive.
  • You can disable sync and delete your backup at any time from within the app or directly from Google Drive.
  • We do not have access to your Google credentials or your Drive contents.

3. On-Device Intelligence

All predictions (cycle length, ovulation estimates) and health insights are generated entirely on your device using local algorithms and TensorFlow.js. No health data is sent to external servers for processing.

4. Notifications

Luna may send local notifications (mood check-ins) if enabled. These are scheduled locally on your device — no push notification servers are involved.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

  • Luna does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • Luna does not collect device identifiers, location data, or usage statistics.
  • Luna does not share any data with third parties.

6. Internet Permission

The app requests Internet permission solely for the optional Google Drive sync feature. If you do not enable sync, no network requests are made.

7. Data Deletion

You can erase all local data at any time from Settings > Erase All Data. This permanently removes all cycle logs, moods, symptoms, and settings from your device. If you have a Google Drive backup, you can also delete it from your Drive.

8. Data Export

You can export all your data as a JSON file at any time from the Settings screen. This allows you to keep a local backup on your device or transfer your data.

9. Children's Privacy

Luna is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
